# ABOUT

<img src="/assets/member/junyu-yang.png" align="right" width="200" />

Mr. Yang Junyu studied under Shi Yunxiang, a disciple of Zhang Daqian, Chen Qi, 
a disciple of Li Kuchan, and Li Xianliang, among others. He received encouragement 
and guidance from Professor Qiao Sen of the Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University, 
and Mr. Gu Baoyu (a disciple of Li Kuchan), a master of traditional Chinese painting. 
He specializes in flower-and-bird painting and splash-ink landscape painting, 
and his works have been collected by Rongbaozhai.

Currently, he is the President of the Guo Meng Shan Hai Academy of Painting and Calligraphy, 
a member of the Chinese Painting, Calligraphy and Seal Carving Artists Association, 
a member of the Zhang Daqian Research Association of Da Feng Tang, the chief painter of the Shengshi Art Museum,
and a guest professor at Qingdao Agricultural University and Shuoyang Postgraduate Entrance Examination Base.
