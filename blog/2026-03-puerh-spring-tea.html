Puerh Spring Tea#

China's agricultural culture has a long history, and the Twenty-Four Solar Terms play a crucial role in it. Farmers plant, harvest, and repeat according to the different solar terms. The definition, harvesting, and production time of Pu-erh spring tea are also closely related to the Twenty-Four Solar Terms.

Tea lovers familiar with Pu-erh spring tea certainly know what Pu-erh spring tea is, right?

Simply put, Pu-erh spring tea refers to tea leaves picked, processed, and produced in the tea-producing areas of Yunnan from the Beginning of Spring (立春) to the Beginning of Summer (立夏).

Knowing the production time of Pu-erh spring tea is far from enough; it's just the basics.

Only when you understand the following questions can you truly be familiar with Pu-erh spring tea.

1. Is spring tea the best quality tea of the year?#

Yes.

Why?

The reasons include: First, from winter to spring, tea trees have a long period of more than 6 months to accumulate nutrients, which can provide sufficient nutrients for the growth of tea leaves, making the internal substances of tea leaves the most abundant in the four seasons of spring, summer, autumn, and winter.

Second, the temperature in spring is relatively neither hot nor cold, which is conducive to the accumulation of a large amount of fresh and sweet substances such as amino acids.

At the same time, rainfall in most areas of Yunnan decreases in spring, which can provide sunny weather for the production of sun-dried raw tea (晒青毛茶) in the Pu-erh spring tea production process.

Finally, just after winter, spring arrives, and everything is just awakening. There are relatively few pests and diseases of tea leaves at this time, which can ensure the quality of the tea leaves.

2. Why is the price of ancient tree spring tea higher in Pu-erh spring tea?#

Things are valued according to their scarcity.

The quantity of ancient tree spring tea is small, especially in some small micro-production areas, the quantity is even smaller.

Ancient tea trees are rare, but there are many people who want to taste them. Supply is less than demand, so the price will naturally be high.

Superior growing environment.

These ancient tea trees grow in deep mountains and forests, gathering the essence of heaven and earth and the wild charm of the forest. The taste is much better than tea leaves grown in other places.

3. Is the most expensive Pu-erh spring tea always the best?#

Price and value are often proportional, and everyone knows this.

But that's not to say you must buy expensive Pu-erh spring tea.

Here are a few suggestions for tea lovers when buying Pu-erh spring tea:

First, understand the general price range of ancient tree spring tea, and then choose to buy according to your preferences.

If you are new to Pu-erh spring tea, you can learn about Pu-erh tea from friends who make tea before buying.

When purchasing, you can gradually advance from entry-level to tasting-level to enthusiast-collector level.

Tea, agreeable to the taste, is precious, and good tea has standards.

Tea lovers often ask, "Is there a standard for good tea? If so, what is the standard?"

The same applies to Pu-erh spring tea; good Pu-erh spring tea also has standards.

That is, the raw materials are of high quality, the tea-making process is exquisite, and the taste can reflect the uniqueness of the tea-producing area.

For example, Pu-erh spring tea from Jingmai Mountain should have the "orchid fragrance" representing the region.

Pu-erh spring tea from the Lincang tea area has a high floral fragrance in the new tea period.

4. How to choose Pu-erh spring tea?#

This is a problem that many tea lovers face. Of course, if you are an experienced tea lover, you must have your own selection experience.

Here are a few suggestions for tea lovers:

First, look, drink, and learn more;

Second, do not blindly pursue rare tea products;

Third, choose tea products according to your preferences;

Fourth, choose a trusted brand tea shop to buy from.

Every spring tea season is an excellent time to taste new spring tea, simply because spring tea only happens once a year.

5. There are also precautions for drinking Pu-erh spring tea.#

Properly drinking Pu-erh spring tea is a very pleasant thing, but you need to pay attention to a few points to better taste the tea.

Spring tea made in the current year is relatively young in terms of age and has a cold nature.

Tea lovers need to consider their own physical condition when drinking it.

If you have an upset stomach recently, it is recommended not to drink raw tea first, but to drink ripened tea to adjust it.

If you are feeling hot recently, you can drink a little spring tea in moderation to reduce the heat.

When drinking Pu-erh spring tea, do not drink it on an empty stomach. It is best to drink it two hours after a meal.

Tea lovers who already have poor sleep quality are not recommended to drink Pu-erh spring tea before going to bed to avoid affecting sleep quality.

Elderly people should also pay attention to their physical condition and drink Pu-erh spring tea appropriately.
